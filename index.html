Our Commitment: Ministry Stewardship Pledge

Masjid Al-Tawheed
(A Ministry Established in Faith, Governed by Principle)

Preamble

We, the stewards, trustees, and faithful workers of Masjid Al-Tawheed, do solemnly affirm our commitment to operate this ministry in righteousness, transparency, and service — never for personal gain or worldly fame, but in full submission to the calling entrusted to us.

1. Purpose Over Profit

  • Reject all forms of commercial entanglement.
  • Refrain from using ministry for personal enrichment.
  • Ensure all assets are spiritually purposed.

2. Transparency Over Secrecy

  • Maintain clear, written records of ministry activity.
  • Answer questions of stewardship with honest accountability.
  • Be open about how all resources are used.

3. Stewardship Over Ownership

  • Operate as stewards, not owners.
  • Treat ministry property as tools of service.
  • Document use in alignment with ecclesiastical jurisdiction.

4. Service Over Status

  • Keep humility at the center.
  • Avoid glorifying individuals above the message.
  • Lift the burdened, not elevate ourselves.

5. Legacy Over Luxury

  • Build systems to sustain faith and spiritual continuity.
  • Raise future stewards with responsibility, not riches.
  • Leave behind a spiritual footprint of transformation.

6. Separation from Corruption

  • Avoid prosperity gospel manipulation and coercion.
  • Never exchange promises of favor for money.
  • Remain separate from secular interference.

7. Alignment with the Original Mandate

  • Seek divine guidance before expanding or acquiring.
  • Operate from divine instruction, not ambition.
  • Pause or pivot if focus is lost.

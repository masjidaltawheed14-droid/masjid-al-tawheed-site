Welcome to Masjid Al-Tawheed

We are a faith-based ministry operating under the ecclesiastical jurisdiction and spiritual mandate of the Most High. This website serves as our official digital record, notice center, and governance portal.

Ecclesiastical Declaration

Masjid Al-Tawheed operates solely under the spiritual jurisdiction of the Most High and functions as a 508(c)(1)(A) religious ministry. We are protected by the First Amendment to the U.S. Constitution, which guarantees freedom of religion, speech, and peaceful assembly.

International Protections

  • Hague Convention (Private International Law) – Recognizes protections for private ecclesiastical entities operating outside state commercial codes.
  • United Nations Charter, Articles 18–20 – Guarantees freedom of conscience, religious expression, and non-interference in spiritual jurisdiction.
  • Vienna Convention on the Law of Treaties – Establishes that private treaties and declarations between sovereigns or faith-based bodies are recognized internationally when not in conflict with jus cogens norms.

We exercise our inherent and irrevocable right to self-govern under divine law, and we maintain all records, trusts, declarations, and operations as sacred and spiritually binding.

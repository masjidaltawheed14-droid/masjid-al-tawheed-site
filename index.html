Welcome to Masjid Al-Tawheed

A sacred ministry standing on divine law,
protected by First Amendment and international treaty authority.

Masjid Al-Tawheed operates as a 508(c)(1)(A) faith-based ministry under the full protections of the First Amendment to the United States Constitution. Our mission is governed by spiritual law, guided by righteous stewardship, and free from commercial entanglement.

Our Ministry Protections Include:

  • First Amendment – U.S. Constitution: “Congress shall make no law respecting an establishment of religion, or prohibiting the free exercise thereof…”
  • 508(c)(1)(A) Status: Recognized under U.S. Tax Code as a church/ministry exempt from registration, taxation, and filing requirements imposed on 501(c)(3) organizations.
  • Universal Declaration of Human Rights – Article 18: “Everyone has the right to freedom of thought, conscience and religion…”
  • United Nations Charter – Article 20: “Everyone has the right to freedom of peaceful assembly and association.”
  • Hague Convention (Private International Law): Upholds the recognition of non-commercial, ecclesiastical entities and their documents across treaty member nations.
  • Vienna Convention on the Law of Treaties – Article 26: “Every treaty in force is binding upon the parties to it and must be performed by them in good faith.”

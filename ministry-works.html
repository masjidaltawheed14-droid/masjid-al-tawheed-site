Ministry Works

Masjid Al-Tawheed engages in active ministry operations including faith-based outreach, education, housing initiatives, health & wellness services, and benevolence programs.

From publications to branded outreach like Apprecilove™ and Project Reclaim™, our ministry extends into the creative sphere to uplift and inspire. Each initiative is governed under trust authority and documented for accountability.

Apprecilove™ is a coined term uniquely identified with Masjid Al-Tawheed and is used in ministry-affiliated works under ecclesiastical copyright.

Project Reclaim™ is a spiritual initiative dedicated to redeeming displaced assets, reversing financial harm, and restoring dignity through ministry-based trust strategies. It operates under ecclesiastical jurisdiction and is protected by common law trademark.

Our mission is rooted in stewardship, guided by principle, and dedicated to building a lasting spiritual legacy.

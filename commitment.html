Ministry Stewardship Pledge

(A Ministry Established in Faith, Governed by Principle)

Preamble — We, the stewards, trustees, and faithful workers of Masjid Al-Tawheed, do solemnly affirm our commitment to operate this ministry in righteousness, transparency, and service — never for personal gain or worldly fame, but in full submission to the calling entrusted to us.

1. Purpose Over Profit

  • Reject all commercial entanglement that compromises the mission.
  • Refrain from using ministry structures for personal enrichment or vanity.
  • Ensure every asset under ministry care is spiritually purposed.

2. Transparency Over Secrecy

  • Maintain clear, written records of ministry activity.
  • Answer stewardship questions with accountability and honesty.
  • Honor the trust of those who stand with the ministry.

3. Stewardship Over Ownership

  • See ourselves as stewards, not owners.
  • Treat ministry property as tools of service, not trophies.
  • Document all ministry use of assets and assignments.

4. Service Over Status

  • Keep humility and servant-leadership at the center.
  • Avoid glorifying individuals above the message.
  • Lift the burdened, the lost, and the seeking.

5. Legacy Over Luxury

  • Build legacy systems, not luxury structures.
  • Raise up future stewards who inherit responsibility, not riches.
  • Leave behind a spiritual footprint marked by transformation.

6. Separation from Corruption

  • Avoid manipulation and coercion.
  • Never exchange promises of divine favor for contributions.
  • Remain separate from secular systems that dilute protections.

7. Alignment with the Original Mandate

  • Return to prayer and counsel before expansion.
  • Operate from divine instruction, not worldly ambition.
  • Pivot if we drift, pause if we lose focus, surrender if we take credit.

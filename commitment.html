Masjid Al-Tawheed (A Ministry Established in Faith, Governed by Principle)

Preamble

We, the stewards, trustees, and faithful workers of Masjid Al-Tawheed, do solemnly affirm our commitment...

1. Purpose Over Profit

  • Reject all forms of commercial entanglement...
  • Refrain from personal enrichment...
  • Ensure all assets are spiritually purposed...

2. Transparency Over Secrecy

  • Maintain clear, written records...
  • Answer stewardship questions with accountability...
  • Honor trust of those who give and serve...

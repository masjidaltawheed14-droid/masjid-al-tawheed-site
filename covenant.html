Covenant

The Sacred Covenant of Masjid Al-Tawheed

This covenant represents the unbroken commitment between the Ministry, its stewards, and the Most High. It affirms unity, responsibility, and spiritual governance under the divine trust bestowed upon Masjid Al-Tawheed.

Principles of the Covenant

  • To uphold righteousness, justice, and truth in all ministry affairs.
  • To protect spiritual jurisdiction under the First Amendment.
  • To preserve the legacy, property, and spiritual works entrusted to us.
  • To stand in unity as one body, one ministry, one purpose.

Affirmation

By this covenant, all stewards, trustees, and members of the ministry acknowledge their sacred responsibility and pledge to walk in integrity, humility, and unwavering devotion. This covenant remains active in perpetuity and stands as a living record under ministry seal.

Trust Framework

Masjid Al-Tawheed operates under a multi-trust framework designed for stewardship, transparency, and spiritual sovereignty. Each trust serves a unique purpose while remaining interconnected under ecclesiastical governance.

Trusts include the Treasury Holding Trust, Ecclesiastical Operations Trust, Housing & Property Trust, Benevolence Trust, Education & Scholarship Trust, Holistic Health & Wellness Trust, Ecclesiastical Copyright Trust, and the Life Insurance Trust.

Together, these trusts form the Ecclesiastical Treasury Framework (ETF), which ensures accountability, lawful recognition, and intergenerational legacy for ministry works.

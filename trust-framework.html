Trust Framework

The Trust Framework of Masjid Al-Tawheed is the foundation of our ministry governance, rooted in First Amendment protections and 508(c)(1)(A) authority.

Foundational Trusts

  • Treasury Holding Trust — Oversees treasury, credit, and inter-trust transactions.
  • Housing & Property Trust — Stewardship of ministry land and properties.
  • Benevolence Trust — Charitable aid and family support.
  • Education & Scholarship Trust — Curriculum, teaching, and training.
  • Holistic Health & Wellness Trust — Nutrition, health, and healing stewardship.
  • Ecclesiastical Copyright Trust — Creative works, writings, and ministry intellectual property.
  • Life Insurance Trust — Generational care and long-term family protection.

Spiritual Credit System

All ministry trusts operate on the issuance and recognition of spiritual credit. These credits are lawful, non-commercial, and fully protected under ecclesiastical jurisdiction.

Jurisdiction & Protection

Masjid Al-Tawheed affirms that all trusts and operations fall solely under ecclesiastical law and First Amendment jurisdiction. No secular authority may impose without voluntary consent.

Legacy & Stewardship

The Trust Framework exists to preserve faith, family, and generational stewardship — ensuring a lasting inheritance of principle and protection.

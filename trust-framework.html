Trust Framework

The Trust Framework of Masjid Al-Tawheed is the foundation of our ministry governance, rooted in First Amendment protections and 508(c)(1)(A) authority.

Foundational Trusts

  • Treasury Holding Trust — Oversees treasury, credit, and inter-trust transactions.
  • Housing & Property Trust — Stewardship of ministry land and properties.
  • Benevolence Trust — Charitable aid and family support.
  • Education & Scholarship Trust — Curriculum, teaching, and training.
  • Holistic Health & Wellness Trust — Health, nutrition, and wellness stewardship.
  • Ecclesiastical Copyright Trust — Creative works and ministry IP.
  • Life Insurance Trust — Generational care and long-term family protection.

Spiritual Credit System

All ministry trusts operate on lawful, non-commercial spiritual credits recognized under ecclesiastical jurisdiction.

Jurisdiction & Protection

All trust operations fall solely under ecclesiastical law and First Amendment jurisdiction. No secular authority may impose without voluntary consent.

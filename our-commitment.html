Our Commitment

Masjid Al-Tawheed
(A Ministry Established in Faith, Governed by Principle)

Ministry Stewardship Pledge

Preamble

We, the stewards, trustees, and faithful workers of Masjid Al-Tawheed, do solemnly affirm our commitment to operate this ministry in righteousness, transparency, and service — never for personal gain or worldly fame, but in full submission to the calling entrusted to us.

1. Purpose Over Profit

  • Reject all forms of commercial entanglement that would compromise the sacred mission.
  • Refrain from using ministry structures for personal enrichment, vanity, or empire-building.
  • Ensure every asset, credit, or possession under ministry care is spiritually purposed, not personally exploited.

2. Transparency Over Secrecy

  • Maintain clear, written records of ministry activity — including spiritual credits, trust movements, and board decisions.
  • Answer questions of stewardship with honest accountability.
  • Honor the trust of those who give, serve, or stand with the ministry by being open about how resources are used.

3. Stewardship Over Ownership

  • See ourselves not as owners, but as stewards of sacred assignments.
  • Treat ministry property — land, vehicles, homes, funds, or creative assets — as tools of service, not trophies of success.
  • Document the ministry’s use of all material items and spiritual assignments in alignment with ecclesiastical jurisdiction.

4. Service Over Status

  • Keep humility and servant-leadership at the center of governance.
  • Avoid titles, appearances, or messaging that glorify individuals above the message.
